**Narration**:

### Scene 3: Verification Interface (120 seconds)

>
> Notice the extraction method and timestamp at the top. This helps you track which documents were processed and when."

### Scene 4: Saving and Proceeding (30 seconds)
**Visual**: Auto-save indicator, proceed button
**Narration**:

## Video 4: Entering Quantities (5-6 minutes)

### Scene 1: Input Methods Overview (30 seconds)
**Visual**: Three tabs - Form, Voice, Image
**Narration**:
> "Enter executed quantities using three flexible methods. Choose the one that fits your workflow best, or use all three together."

### Scene 2: Form Input Method (90 seconds)
**Visual**: Form interface, number inputs, validation, totals
**Narration**:
> "The Form Input method is straightforward. Each work order item has a quantity field. Enter numbers directly using your keyboard.
>
> Watch the running totals update in real-time. The app validates your input immediately - negative numbers are rejected, and warnings appear if quantities exceed 110% of the work order.
>
> The form auto-saves every 30 seconds, so your progress is never lost."

### Scene 3: Voice Input Method (120 seconds)
**Visual**: Microphone button, transcript display, parsing, confirmation
**Narration**:
> "Voice Input is perfect for hands-free operation. Tap the microphone button and speak naturally.
>
> Say commands like:
> - 'Item twelve, fifteen numbers'
> - 'Item number twelve, quantity fifteen'
> - In Hindi: 'बारह नंबर, पंद्रह संख्या'
>
> The app displays your transcript and parses the command. If ambiguous, you'll see a confirmation dialog. Accepted quantities are highlighted in green."

### Scene 4: Image Input Method (150 seconds)
**Visual**: Camera capture, handwriting recognition, extraction results
**Narration**:
> "Image Input extracts quantities from handwritten notes. Tap the camera button to capture a photo of your notes.
>
> The app uses advanced handwriting recognition to identify item numbers and quantities. It looks for patterns like:
> - '12: 15' (item 12, quantity 15)
> - '12 - 15'
> - '15 for item 12'
>
> Extracted data appears with confidence scores. Low-confidence items are flagged for your review. You can capture multiple photos - the app intelligently resolves conflicts.
>
> This method is ideal when you have field notes or supervisor-approved quantities on paper."

### Scene 5: Review and Validation (30 seconds)
**Visual**: Validation errors, warnings, corrections
**Narration**:
> "Before generating the bill, review all quantities. The app highlights any validation errors in red. Warnings appear in yellow. Fix any issues before proceeding."

## Video 8: Troubleshooting (2-3 minutes)

### Scene 1: Common Issues (90 seconds)
**Visual**: Problem scenarios and solutions
**Narration**:
> "Common issues and solutions:
>
> **PDF extraction fails**: Check file size (max 10MB), ensure PDF is not password-protected, try re-scanning with better quality
>
> **Voice input not working**: Check microphone permissions, ensure browser supports Web Speech API, try Chrome or Edge
>
> **Offline sync stuck**: Check internet connection, clear browser cache, refresh the app
>
> **Bill generation errors**: Verify all quantities are entered, check contractor information is complete, ensure no validation errors"
